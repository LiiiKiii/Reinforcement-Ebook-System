论文标题: Reflections on the Fifth International Conference on Women in Physics
作者: Chandralekha Singh
摘要: This article describes reflections on the Fifth International Conference on Women in Physics which was a conference attended by 215 female physicists and a few male physicists from 49 different countries. The article focuses on the barriers that women face in their professional advancement in physics and the extent to which the situation is different in various countries.
