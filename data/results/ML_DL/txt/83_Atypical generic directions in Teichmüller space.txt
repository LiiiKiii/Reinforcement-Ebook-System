论文标题: Atypical generic directions in Teichmüller space
作者: Matthew Gentry Durham, Chenxi Wu, Kejia Zhu
摘要: Motivated by geometrically capturing generic directions in Teichmüller space -- that is, tracking rays for random walks of the mapping class group -- we use work of Chaika--Masur--Wolf and Durham--Zalloum to construct the first examples of a sublinearly-Morse Teichmüller geodesic rays with minimal non-uniquely ergodic vertical foliations.
