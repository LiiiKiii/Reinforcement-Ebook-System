论文标题: CuBr2-A New Multiferroic Material with High Critical Temperature
作者: L. Zhao, T. L. Hung, C. C. Li, Y. Y. Chen, M. K. Wu
摘要: A new multiferroic material, CuBr2, is reported for the first time. CuBr2 has not only a high transition temperature (close to liquid nitrogen temperature) but also low dielectric loss and strong magnetoelectric coupling. These findings reveal the importance of anion effects in the search for the high temperature multiferroics materials among these low-dimensional spin systems.
